Walt Disney's Creative Strategy - Brainstorming Method

History
Condensed versions Short on time? Try these variations...
Group of different projects using Dreamer/Realist (pair/share)
  1. One person shares their interests/inspiration.
  2. Everyone pitch rapid-fire ideas on the theme ("as dreamers"). Continue discussing ideas.
  3. After one or two of the ideas start to seam feasible then discuss Realist perspectives.
  4. That person should take notes and continue research. Later they can be the critic.
